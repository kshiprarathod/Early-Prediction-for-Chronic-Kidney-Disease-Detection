About CKD Prediction System

Project Overview

The Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) Prediction System is an AI-powered web app designed to assist in the early detection of chronic kidney disease. It analyzes patient health parameters to predict CKD likelihood, enabling timely intervention and improved outcomes.

Key Scenarios

Early Detection

Identify abnormal creatinine and albumin levels from routine health checkups.

Disease Severity Prediction

Predict CKD severity based on biomarkers like GFR, hemoglobin, and serum creatinine.

Progression Monitoring

Track key biomarkers to monitor disease progression and adjust treatments.
